fly me to the moon is a 3D-animated application driven by webGL technologies from Cesium and Three.js. It's a client-side app that attempts to provide both a visual and informational experience.

after each scene, please click the blue home icon to reset the camera.

click fly me home to arrive at your current location.
click fly me anywhere to visit some of our planets most spectacular locations.
click fly me to the moon to engage in a Three.js-driven flight animation.

use the search bar to fly anywhere on earth.
use the map icon to swap out 3D-ariel view for 2D-terrain view.

visit the source code for more details about this application.
